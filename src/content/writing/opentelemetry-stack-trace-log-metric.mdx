The three observability signals — traces, logs, metrics — have historically
lived in different vendors with different query languages, different sampling
philosophies, and different costs. The OTel stack lets one ingest pipeline
feed all three, and Grafana's dashboards can correlate across them with
TraceID-as-foreign-key. The migration is non-trivial; the steady state pays
for itself the first time someone debugs a production incident at 2 a.m.
