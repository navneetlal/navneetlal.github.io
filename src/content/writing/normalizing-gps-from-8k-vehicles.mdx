Every fleet customer brought their own tracking stack — some with polished
HTTP APIs, some with devices that still spoke a custom binary protocol over
2G. Add in customer-internal trip-management systems and third-party
carrier APIs, and "normalization" stops being a string-transform exercise
and starts being a noise-filtering + state-machine problem.
