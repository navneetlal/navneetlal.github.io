The shape of the problem: a multi-tenant Mongo cluster already running at ~70%
of its IOPS budget, and a downstream analytics pipeline that needs every change
in near-real-time. The naïve thing — polling collections on a timer — pushes
load somewhere that has none to spare. Debezium's oplog tailing approach is
read-only from Mongo's perspective and, done right, costs the source almost
nothing.
