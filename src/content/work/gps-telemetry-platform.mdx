Mid-mile logistics tracking for enterprise quick-commerce customers — ingesting
GPS telemetry from 8–10k vehicles across Indian metros.

## Problem

Customer-supplied fleet trackers ranged from polished APIs to flaky devices on
2G. Pings arrived 10–30s apart depending on motion state. Cross-source
normalization had to happen before any downstream system saw the data.

## Approach

- Built the org's first CI/CD pipeline (Jenkins, solo) and migrated 7–10
  services to EKS — deploy freq 1/week → 2–3/day.
- Integration layer over carrier APIs, customer-internal systems, and
  third-party fleet/contract-management tools.
- Cross-source GPS normalization + noise filtering as a first-class pipeline
  stage.
- Replaced custom auth with Keycloak as the customer-facing SSO layer.

## Outcome

Customer onboarding unlocked regardless of the customer's existing tracking
stack. 40–80M events/day at peak.
