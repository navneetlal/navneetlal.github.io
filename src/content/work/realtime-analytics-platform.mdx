The company's first analytics platform — built end-to-end from CDC source
through to customer-facing dashboards and an ML/historical lakehouse.

## Problem

Analytics ran on the multi-tenant Mongo cluster, queueing behind transactional
write load with ~50 collections per tenant. Dashboards were minutes-stale; ad-hoc
analytical queries were not viable at all.

## Approach

- **CDC stream:** Debezium tails Mongo oplogs into Kafka, one topic per source
  collection per tenant.
- **Hot path:** Kafka → StarRocks for sub-second customer dashboards.
- **Cold path:** Kafka → Spark Structured Streaming → Iceberg lakehouse with
  bronze/silver/gold tiers, orchestrated by Airflow.

## Outcome

10–15 TB of historical analytics data off the OLTP store, ms-latency on the
live dashboards, and a lakehouse footprint that ML/data-science consume
without touching the multi-tenant Mongo.
