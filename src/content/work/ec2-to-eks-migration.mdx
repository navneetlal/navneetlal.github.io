Moved 45 microservices off long-lived EC2/systemd hosts and onto EKS — across
customer tenants and 250+ instances — without a maintenance window.

## Problem

Deploys ran for 90 minutes. Capacity was provisioned by hand; off-peak headroom
sat idle 24/7. Restarting a single service required SRE pager-time.

## Approach

- Rebuilt service Docker images on a shared base, fronted by Helm charts.
- Tenant-aware deployment pipelines on Jenkins, gated by smoke tests.
- Authored runbooks SRE owned for the customer rollout.

## Outcome

Deploys 90 → 20 minutes. Infra cost −30% from autoscaling-driven right-sizing.
First time the platform could scale horizontally on demand.
